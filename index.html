risks of a digital project that may lead it to fail

One of the risks that could happen is that you could be in the middle of a project and halfway through your project a new model could be released which could make your website or app not work as you intended on the new device which could lead you to restart the app or website so it works correctly on the latest device and the devices before. Another risk is that while you are in mid build for your website or application, another company could come out with a new feature that might drive away your customers which may lead for your website or application to fail.
