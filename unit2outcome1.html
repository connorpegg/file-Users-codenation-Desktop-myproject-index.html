If there are flashing images it will cause a fit for someone who has epilepsy or if someone has slight sight loss and they can’t zoom in on your content then they can’t read what is on the website they are visiting or if it is a video on a website and a person is deaf and there are no subtitles then they can’t watch the video since they can’t get the same experience as everyone else.
